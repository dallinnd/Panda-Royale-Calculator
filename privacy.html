Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Panda Royale Calculator ("the App") is provided as a free service. Your privacy is paramount; this policy explains our data practices.

1. Data Collection & Storage

The App does not collect personal data. All game history, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device via LocalStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

2. Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or trade any information with third parties. No analytics or advertising trackers are used in this App.

3. Permissions

The App does not require access to your location, contacts, or camera.

4. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer via the official project repository.
